# Saved Views and Favorites

Aperio allows you to customize your dashboard by marking your most frequently used resources as "Favorites" and saving custom table configurations as "Saved Views".

## Favorites

If you find yourself accessing the same resource multiple times (e.g., `Users` or `Orders`), you can pin it to the top of your sidebar for instant access.

### How to use Favorites

1. Navigate to any resource in your API.
2. In the top header next to the resource's name, click the **Star** icon.
3. The resource is immediately added to a new **Favorites** section pinned at the top of your left sidebar.
4. To remove a resource, simply click the star again either on the resource's page or from within the Favorites sidebar section.

Favorites are persisted locally in your browser for the specific API specification you are currently viewing.

---

## Saved Views

Tables in Aperio provide powerful filtering and sorting capabilities. Instead of re-configuring a table each time you open the dashboard, you can save your current table state as a Saved View.

### How to create a Saved View

1. Navigate to a resource with an auto-generated table (e.g., a GET collection endpoint).
2. Apply any combination of sorting, global filters, and column visibility to suit your needs.
3. Click the **Save View** button located in the table's header next to the Refresh button.
4. Enter a memorable name (e.g., "Active Users" or "Recent Orders") and click **Save View** in the dialog.

### How to load a Saved View

Once you have saved a view for a particular resource, a new dropdown menu will appear in that table's header.
Simply select your view from the dropdown, and Aperio will instantly restore your saved sorting, column visibility, and filters. You can always revert to the unfiltered state by selecting **Default View**.
