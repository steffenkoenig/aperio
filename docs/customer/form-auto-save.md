# Form Auto-Save and Drafts

Aperio helps you prevent data loss by automatically saving your form progress when you're crafting complex API requests.

## How it Works

When you fill out form fields for **POST**, **PUT**, or **PATCH** operations, Aperio continuously saves your changes behind the scenes.

If you accidentally close your browser tab, navigate to a different dashboard page, or refresh your browser, your data will not be lost. Simply return to the same endpoint you were working on, and you will see a "Draft restored" notification. Your form will be populated exactly as you left it.

## Discarding a Draft

If you want to clear your saved progress and start fresh, look for the **Discard Draft** button next to the primary submit button. Clicking this will instantly clear the form and delete the local draft.

## Completion

Once you successfully execute the API request by submitting the form, Aperio automatically clears the draft so that your next request starts with a clean slate.
